What is Lively?
An app for livestock was never made in india because there was no means for the farmers and the livestock managers to use it. As our country is progressing and internet and other facilities can now be used by people living in villages as well. Now we can provide technical help to those in need there. One such help needed is livestock management. Our app Aims to provide the means to fulfil the needs of the livestock managers and farmers.

SERVICES WE OFFER

Inventory

Stores data about Farm Animals like type, breed, number

Animal Welfare

Stores data about animal health condition, diseases

Community Connect

A forum to connect with doctors, ngos and farmers

Farm Affairs

Shows the farm performance based onlike health, gender
